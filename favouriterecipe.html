A Slice of Heaven: Bourbon Pecan Pie

I bet your mouth is watering just from reading the title of this recipe. Well get your Maker's Mark in hand and even have a little swig before cause you taste buds ain't ready for the heavenly goodness about to be made. Let's get cookin'!

Ingredients to reach Heaven

For the crust

  • 3/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 7 tablespoons unsalted butter, chilled
  • 3-4 tablespoons ice water

For pie filling

  • 2 eggs
  • 4 yolks
  • 5 ounces sugar
  • 8.5 ounces dark corn syrup
  • 3 ounces melted butter
  • 1.5 ounce bourbon

For each unbaked pie shell

  • 3.5 ounces chocolate chips on bottom (melt onto pie crust before proceeding
  • 7 ounces pecan, on top
  • 21 ounces filling, poured over

Directions to make Heaven

  1. Whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt to make the dough.
  2. Cube the butter, and add it into your flour mixture.
  3. Use your hands to cut the butter into the flour, until pea-sized crumbs form, but still leaving a few pieces of visible butter.
  4. Add your water, a tablespoon at a time, until the dough comes together into a ball without being wet.
  5. Wrap in plastic wrap, shape into a disk, and chill for at least 3 hours.
  6. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F.
  7. Roll out your pie crust on a generously floured work surface, and place it in your 8-inch pie tin, crimping the edges and cutting off any excess.
  8. Place in freezer for 10 minutes or so while you prepare the filling.
  9. Whisk eggs, yolk, and sugar together until combined and smooth. Add remaining ingredients and whisk again until smooth. Pour into pie shell.
  10. Bake the pie at 325 degrees F for 45 mins, or until puffed and set.
